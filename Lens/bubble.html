bubble trouble

swayed by nostalgia, my partner and I wanted a spin-off of the computer game Bubble Trouble. each time the main character shoots his arrow and pops a face, the bubble splits into two bubbles of half the size, exponentially threatening his life. we experimented with the Model-View-Controller structure, Pygame, and the physics of bouncing balls.
