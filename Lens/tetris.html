terminal tetris

in exploring the applications of C for our Software Systems class, my team and I replicated the timeless game of Tetris, but designed it with the working developer in mind. our version runs straight from the command line and even includes a "boss mode" that pauses and disguises the game for whenever the boss walks by. warning: this game is not to be played by recovering tetris addicts.
