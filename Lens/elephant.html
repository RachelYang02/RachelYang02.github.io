biomimicry play experience

for Design Nature, we engaged fourth graders with a fun, challenging, consistent game that promoted both competition and teamwork. we based our game off of the interaction mother elephants have with their children during feeding. we used a claw and slingshot system to allow the momma to pick up "food," or ping pong balls, and shoot them to her trapped child to give her enough energy to break out and escape.
