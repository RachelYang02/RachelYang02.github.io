text mining + word art

this project was centered around the idea of text parsing. I am absolutely enraptured by movie posters, and how powerful they can be, so I wrote a program in Python that parses through movie scripts and generates a corresponding word cloud of its most frequent words. it then overlaps this cloud onto an iconic image from the movie. through this project, I experimented with Pickle.
